About Phoenix Club

Founded in 1907, Phoenix Club is one of the oldest and most prestigious social clubs in Calcutta. Our goal is to provide a warm, welcoming environment where individuals of all ages can come together and enjoy various activities, build friendships, and enrich their lives.

Whether you're interested in sports, arts, fitness, or socializing, Phoenix Club offers an all-inclusive range of activities for every interest.

- James Gomez, President
